-== CONTROLS ==-
MOVE = ARROW KEYS / JOYSTICK
ATTACK = CTRL / LEFT MOUSE CLICK / FIRE1
GET HIT / TAKE DAMAGE = K

ATTACK 1 HAPPENS WHEN NOT MOVING
ATTACK 2 HAPPENS WHEN MOVING
IF "K" IS PRESSED SEVERAL TIMES, DEATH ANIMATION WILL PLAY
